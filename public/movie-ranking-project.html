The Best Movies according to Juan cruz

My top 3 favourites movies

Gladiator

I used to be passionate about movies about ancient Rome and especially about gladiators when I was a child. All thanks to this movie.
I really liked the epic battles of Maximus in the Roman Colosseum and the plot in general.

Your Name

If you enjoy anime and romantic genre, believe me, this one is a perfect mix for you.
It's a classic in the anime genre of this type.

Hacksaw Ridge

It's a story based on real events. No words can describe it.
The flood of emotions this movie generates in me is indescribable.
