The Best Movies According to Juan Cruz

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Gladiator

It's my favorite movie. It features one of the most inspirational quotes I've ever heard in my life: "What we do in life echoes in eternity."

Dragon Ball Z: The Legendary Super Saiyan

It's one of the first Dragon Ball movies I ever watched, and the antagonist is truly memorable.

Berserk (1997)

Probably the most controversial on this list due to its omission of important details from the manga. However, I have to admit I loved the art style, and in my opinion, it's the best adaptation.
